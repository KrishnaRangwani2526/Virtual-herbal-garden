Scientific Name: Cinnamomum verum

Common Names: Cinnamon, Dalchini

Family: Lauraceae

Parts Used: Bark, leaves, oil

Rasa: Pungent, sweet

Guna: Light, dry

Virya: Heating

Vipaka: Sweet

Dosha Effect: Balances Kapha & Vata

Medicinal Uses: Digestive aid, anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial

Benefits: Improves circulation, regulates blood sugar, antioxidant

Precautions: High doses may cause liver issues; avoid in pregnancy
