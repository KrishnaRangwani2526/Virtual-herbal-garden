Name: Turmeric

Scientific Name: Curcuma longa

Common Names: Haldi, Haridra

Family: Zingiberaceae

Parts Used: Rhizome

Rasa: Bitter, pungent

Guna: Light, dry

Virya: Heating

Vipaka: Pungent

Dosha Effect: Balances Kapha & Vata, may increase Pitta

Medicinal Uses: Anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, liver support

Benefits: Wound healing, digestion, blood purification, skin glow

Precautions: High doses may aggravate acidity (Pitta)
